Privacy Policy — ಜನನಿ · Janani (English for Amma)

Effective date: 6 July 2026

Summary: this app collects nothing. ಈ ಆ್ಯಪ್ ನಿಮ್ಮ ಯಾವುದೇ ಮಾಹಿತಿಯನ್ನು ಸಂಗ್ರಹಿಸುವುದಿಲ್ಲ.

What data we collect

None. Janani has no server, no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no trackers. We never see your name, your progress, or anything else.

What stays on your device

Your name, learning goals, lesson progress, streaks, and settings are stored only in your device's local browser storage. They never leave your phone. Uninstalling the app (or clearing site data) deletes them permanently.

Optional AI tutor

The app has an optional "Guru" AI tutor that is off by default. It works only if you enter your own Anthropic API key in Settings. If you do, the sentences you type or speak in the Guru chat are sent directly from your device to Anthropic (anthropic.com) to generate a reply, under Anthropic's privacy policy. Your key is stored only on your device. If you never enter a key, the app makes no network requests at all after loading.

Speech

Audio playback uses your device's built-in text-to-speech. The optional microphone input uses your device's built-in speech recognition (which on Android may be processed by Google per your device settings). The app itself never records or stores audio.

Children

The app is designed for adult learners and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes & contact

Any change to this policy will be posted on this page. Questions: open an issue at github.com/jarvisss007/janani-english.
